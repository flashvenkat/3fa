Zero Trust Architecture

Traditional passwords are no longer enough. SECURE 3FA implements a rigorous three-factor authentication pipeline to ensure absolute certainty of identity before granting access to sensitive systems.

How It Works

Our system uses a sequential verification workflow. An attacker would need to compromise your mind (password), physically steal your mobile device (TOTP), and perfectly replicate your live physical appearance (Biometrics) simultaneously to breach the system. This makes unauthorized access virtually impossible.

🧠

1. Knowledge

Something you know.

The first line of defense. We use highly secure, salted cryptographic hashing to verify your standard credentials (username and password) without ever storing the plain text.

📱

2. Possession

Something you have.

A cryptographic secret is shared with your Authenticator app. It generates a unique 6-digit Time-Based One-Time Password (TOTP) that expires every 30 seconds.

👁️

3. Inherence

Something you are.

The ultimate validator. Our AI-driven neural threat detection extracts 128 unique facial measurements from your webcam to mathematically prove your physical presence.
