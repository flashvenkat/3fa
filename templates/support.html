Support Center

Need help with your 3FA setup? Check our frequently asked questions below or contact our security team.

I lost my Authenticator app. How do I log in?

If you have lost access to your Factor 2 device, you must contact your IT administrator to reset your TOTP seed. For security reasons, this cannot be bypassed.

The facial recognition isn't detecting my face.

Ensure you are in a well-lit environment and looking directly into the camera. Do not wear sunglasses or heavy face coverings during the biometric scan.

My account is locked. What do I do?

Our rate-limiting security locks accounts for 15 minutes after 5 consecutive failed attempts across any factor. Please wait 15 minutes and try again.
